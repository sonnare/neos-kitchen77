About IM

Influencer Mania is a platform that provides others with a platform. An opportunity for many aspiring content creators to bask in the spotlight and become one of the stars. It is driven solely by thousands of dreams and hopes. It recognizes talent and it does its best to find ways to use it to its fullest potential. Influencer Mania is a non-profit organization dedicated only to helping and improving social media talent. With the combination of both the user and this organization the results may be amazing.



Funding

We have partnered with countless sponsors to make this into reality. The majority of which are influencers/content creators themselves. They contribute their time and finances toward the course. There is also a requirement to donate a certain amount towards this foundation, this requirement applies to content creators with more than 500 thousand Followers on a platform and earning more than 20 thousand rand a month. They donate 2.3 percent of their funds towards helping people to reach their goals.



Application and Status check

To qualify for an application, you need to have an account that has been existing for at least 31 days, A minimum of 6 videos/content posts, consistent upload times (2 times every week at least) and Content that is unique and creative in your own way. Creators with more than 30 thousand Followers on any platform do not qualify for an application. How to apply? Submit a picture of your pages, six of your best content, Your insight analysis for the past 30 days, your name, surname, age and contact information. Submit a document essay of at least 1000 words explaining your content, your ideas, your uniqueness, where you want to reach, what you will need and why you deserve to be considered for this opportunity. Submit your application and check the status with a 48-hour period, and you will receive feedback on the status of your application.



Business

Once you have been accepted, we will evaluate what you need and provide you with the necessary training, advice and equipment you may need to move forward. We will provide you with space (depending on your content) to film/record and become creative with high-end equipment. You will gain authorized Access to company equipment yet the proceedings in which you make while under our contract will be managed by the company and distributed to you. Accepted applicants will also get the chance to talk to their favorite content creator for an hour and get advice from them (it will be filmed for the site).
